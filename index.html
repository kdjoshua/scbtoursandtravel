About Uganda

Uganda is ‘gifted by nature’. It is a home to over 14 primate species, nearly 150 mammals and over 1,000 bird species. It has spectacular scenery and this makes Uganda a paradise to those who loves nature. Apart from just amazing nature,Uganda has more to offer including and it includes the following.

  • 01 Outdoor activities

    Uganda has various outdoor activities like hiking, kayaking, white water rafting, mountain biking among other

  • 02Diverse Culture

    With over 50 tribes all with their own customs and traditions. Visit schools, farms and cultural/ religious sites or try your hand at cookery, drumming or craft making. All with the famously friendly and welcoming Ugandan people.

  • 02Rest and relaxtion

    After all that, you might be in need of a little rest and relaxation and Uganda has that covered too. Why not spend a couple of days at the beach, in the Sesse Islands, relaxing in the sunshine and indulging in a little pampering in an exclusive spa?

  • 03Thriving Mmodern Culture

    Which a tour of Kampala will quickly reveal as you visit some of the trendiest shops, galleries, cafes, bars and restaurants and dance the night away in some of the hottest clubs.
